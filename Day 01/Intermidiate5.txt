PROBLEM 5 :

Input Format
Constraints
Length of Arrays should be between 1 to 1000.
Output Format
Sample Input
7
1 2 3 1 2 4 1
2 1 3 1 5 2 2
Sample Output
[1, 1, 2, 2, 3]
